## ⚙️ System Requirements

Before you start, make sure your system meets the following requirements:

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 or later
- **RAM:** 4 GB minimum
- **Storage:** At least 200 MB of free space
- **Graphics:** Any graphics card supporting WebGL
- **Browser:** Latest version of Google Chrome, Firefox, or Microsoft Edge

## 🎨 How to Use AqadilOS

Once you have installed AqadilOS, opening and navigating it is simple:

1. Launch the application by double-clicking the icon.
2. You will see the main dashboard where various sections appear.
3. Use the menu to switch between different aspects of your portfolio, such as projects, skills, and contact information.
4. You can add projects by clicking the "Add Project" button and filling in the necessary details.
5. To update your resume, click on the "Resume" section and follow the prompts to input your information.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

- **Feature Access Issues:** Ensure you have an active internet connection if you are trying to access online features.
